Privacy Policy for MyDay Astro

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

At MyDay Astro, we are committed to providing you with accurate astrological insights while protecting your personal data. This policy explains what our app collects and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

To generate your astrology charts and manage your account, we collect the following:

  • Google Authentication: We use Google Sign-In for secure access. We only collect your name and email address. We do not access or collect your Google profile picture.
  • Astrological Data: To create your personalized charts, we ask you to provide your Date of Birth, Time of Birth, and Place of Birth. This data is essential for the app's core functionality.
  • App Interaction Data: We store your saved readings, notification time preference, language preference, and in-app feedback (such as insight helpfulness ratings and accuracy ratings). This data is used to personalize your experience and improve the app.
  • Feedback Data: If you use our optional "Feedback via WhatsApp" feature, we receive your phone number and message content voluntarily.

2. Device Permissions

Notifications: Our app may send you daily insight notifications at a time of your choosing. Upon first use, we will ask for your permission to send these. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

Firebase Database: All your astrological data and profile information are stored securely in Google Firebase. This ensures your data is saved if you switch devices. We use industry-standard encryption provided by Firebase to ensure your data is safe.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • Google Sign-In (Authentication)
  • Firebase (Database, Cloud Messaging, and Cloud Functions)
  • Meta (Facebook) SDK (Ad attribution and install measurement - used to measure the performance of our app promotion campaigns. This SDK may collect your device's advertising identifier.)
  • WhatsApp (Optional user-initiated support)

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can update or delete your birth details at any time within the app. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time using the Delete account option in the app (Settings - My Account).

If you cannot use the app but still want to delete your account, you can submit a request using our request form. You must use the same email address that you use to sign in to the app. Open the form here: MyDay Astro - Account and Data Deletion Request.
