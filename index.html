Privacy Policy for MyDay Astro

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

Hi, and thanks for using MyDay Astro. We built this little app to give you a moment of astrological guidance each morning — and to do that, you trust us with some personal details. We don't take that lightly. So here's an honest, plain-English rundown of what we collect, why we need it, and what we'll never do with it. No dense legal jargon — just the real picture.

1. What We Collect

To build your birth chart and write your daily readings, here's what we actually collect — and why:

  • Your sign-in details: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we get your name and email — and that's it. If you use Apple's "Hide My Email," we only ever see the private relay address Apple gives us. We never touch your social media profile picture.
  • Your birth details: Your date, time, and place of birth, plus your gender. These are the heart of the app — without them, there's simply no chart to read. This is the one thing we genuinely need from you.
  • What you do in the app: The readings you save, your preferred notification time, your language, and any ratings or feedback you give us. We use this to tailor your experience and to figure out what's working and what isn't.
  • How the app is performing: Some basic, behind-the-scenes analytics about which screens and features get used, plus diagnostic info that helps us spot and fix problems.
  • Your device basics: A notification token (so we can actually send your daily push), your device type, and which app version you're on — the practical stuff that keeps things running.
  • Advertising identifier: Only if you allow it, we use your device's advertising identifier to see whether our app promotions are reaching the right people. You can say no, or change your mind later, in your device settings.
  • WhatsApp messages: Completely optional — if you reach out through our "Feedback via WhatsApp" button, we'll naturally see your number and whatever you write to us.

2. Permissions We Ask For

Notifications: We'd love to send you a gentle daily insight at a time you pick. We'll ask before sending the first one, and you can switch them off whenever you like in your device settings.

Advertising and tracking: Before we — or the Meta (Facebook) tools we use — ever touch your advertising identifier, we ask. Say no and the app works exactly the same. You can change your mind any time in your device settings.

3. Where Your Data Lives

Your chart and profile are stored securely with Google Firebase, which is also what lets your data follow you if you switch phones. Everything travels over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and is encrypted at rest, with access controls on top. We'll be honest: no app on earth can promise data is 100% unbreakable — but we take real, sensible steps to keep yours safe.

4. The Services We Rely On

We're a small app, so a few trusted services do some of the heavy lifting. Here's exactly who they are and what they see:

  • Google & Apple Sign-In — how you log in securely.
  • Firebase — stores your data and powers notifications, our backend, and basic analytics.
  • OpenAI — writes your daily readings. To do this we send it only your first name and the astrological details worked out from your chart. We do not send your email, phone number, or your actual birth date, time, or place.
  • VedicAstroAPI — the astrology engine that calculates your chart and the day's planetary movements. We send it your birth details (date, time, and place) to do the math.
  • Meta (Facebook) tools — only to measure whether our app ads are working. They may read your advertising identifier, and only with your permission.
  • WhatsApp — only if you choose to message us for support.

5. Keeping and Deleting Your Data

We hold onto your data only while you're actually using the app — that's what lets us show your saved readings and history. The moment you delete your account, your profile and astrological data are wiped from our active database, and any leftover copies in backups or logs are gone within 30 days.

You can edit or clear your birth details any time in the app. And whenever you want, you can permanently delete your account and everything tied to it from Settings → My Account → Delete account.

Can't open the app but still want out? Fill in our deletion request form using the same email you signed in with: MyDay Astro — Account and Data Deletion Request. For anything else, just email us below.

6. Children

MyDay Astro is made for a general, grown-up audience — it isn't aimed at children. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 without a parent or guardian's consent. If you think a child has shared their details with us, email us and we'll delete them quickly.

7. You're in Control

We only process your data because you've chosen to share it with us, and because the app needs it to work. You can withdraw that consent any time — just delete your account.

Depending on where you live (including under the EU/UK GDPR and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you also have the right to see your data, correct it, delete it, get a copy of it, take back your consent, and complain to your local data protection authority. Most of this you can do right inside the app — or just reach out to us.

8. Data Crossing Borders

Some of the services we use (like Firebase, OpenAI, and Meta) run on servers outside your country, including in the United States. So by using the app, your data may travel to and be handled in places with different privacy laws. We count on these providers' own safeguards to protect it along the way.

9. Updates to This Policy

If we change anything meaningful here, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top, give you a heads-up in the app where it makes sense, and ask for your consent again where the law requires it. It's worth a glance now and then.

10. We Don't Sell Your Data

Plain and simple: we do not sell your personal data to anyone, and we don't share it for cross-context behavioural advertising. That's a promise.
