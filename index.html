Privacy Policy for MyDay Astro

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

At MyDay Astro, we are committed to providing you with accurate astrological insights while protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

To generate your astrology charts and manage your account, we collect the following:

  • Google Authentication: We use Google Sign-In for secure access. We only collect your name and email address. We do not access or collect your Google profile picture.
  • Astrological Data: To create your personalized charts, we ask you to provide your Date of Birth, Time of Birth, and Place of Birth. This data is essential for the app's core functionality.
  • Feedback Data: If you use our optional "Feedback via WhatsApp" feature, we receive your phone number and message content voluntarily.

2. Device Permissions

Notifications: Our app sends daily morning notifications with your astrology updates. Upon first use, we will ask for your explicit permission to send these. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

Firebase Database: All your astrological data and profile information are stored securely in Google Firebase. This ensures your data is saved if you switch devices. We use industry-standard encryption provided by Firebase to ensure your data is safe.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • Google Play Services (Core functionality)
  • Google Sign-In (Authentication)
  • Firebase (Database and Performance)
  • WhatsApp (Optional user-initiated support)

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can update or delete your birth details at any time within the app. You can also request the permanent deletion of your account and all stored data by emailing us.
